How it works
How we get you sponsored
You stay focused on tennis; we run the commercial process — privately, until there's a deal worth announcing.
1

Share your reach and what a sponsor would get. Nothing is made public — you never have to advertise that you're "looking".

2

Announce the win

When it's signed, it joins the news feed — measured with QR, promo codes and in-app tracking, which drives renewals.
